router-link默认html解析成a标签；如果要修改为div标签，只需要添加属性tag="div"
<router-link to="/menu"  tag="div"  class="nav-link">菜单</router-link>

绑定链接 :to接收链接
<router-link :to="{name:'homeLink'}" class="nav-link">主页</router-link>
